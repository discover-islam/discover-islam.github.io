The 99 Beautiful Names of Allah

\"To Allah belong the Most Beautiful Names\" (Quran 20:8). The 99 names of Allah describe His perfect attributes and serve as a guide to knowing the Divine.

'Allah! There is no god but He! To Him belongs the Most Beautiful Names.' (Quran 20:8). The 99 names of Allah — known as Al-Asma Al-Husna — are not merely labels but windows into the Divine nature, each revealing an aspect of God's infinite perfection.

Ar-Rahman (The Most Merciful) — The One who has an abundance of mercy for both believers and non-believers in this world, and especially for believers in the Hereafter. Ar-Rahim (The Most Compassionate) — The One who has great mercy especially for believers.

Al-Malik (The King) — The One with complete and perfect Dominion. Al-Quddus (The Holy One) — The One who is pure from any imperfection. As-Salam (The Source of Peace) — The One who is free from every imperfection and grants peace to His creation.

Al-Khaliq (The Creator) — The One who brings everything from non-existence into existence. Al-Bari' (The Originator) — The Creator who has the power to turn the entities. Al-Musawwir (The Fashioner) — The One who forms His creatures in different forms.

Al-Ghaffar (The Forgiving) — The One who forgives the sins of His servants time and time again. Al-Wahhab (The Bestower) — The One who is Generous in giving without any return. Ar-Razzaq (The Provider) — The One who provides for all of creation.

Al-Alim (The All-Knowing) — Who knows all things. Al-Qadir (The All-Powerful) — Who has complete power. Al-Hayy (The Ever-Living) — Who never dies. Al-Qayyum (The Self-Sustaining) — Who sustains all of creation. These names invite us to know God deeply and build a personal relationship with Him through worship, gratitude, and reliance.
