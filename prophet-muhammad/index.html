Prophet Muhammad

\"And We have sent you not but as a mercy for the worlds.\" [Quran 21:107]. Discover the life, character, teachings, and legacy of the Final Prophet.

10 Articles in This Section

Article 1
Know the Prophet Muhammad
\"And We have sent you not but as a mercy for the worlds.\" [Quran 21:107]. An introduction to the life and mission of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
Article 2
The Finality of Prophethood
The Islamic belief that Muhammad (peace be upon him) is the final prophet and messenger of God, completing and sealing all previous divine messages.
Article 3
Description of the Prophet Muhammad
A detailed description of the Prophet Muhammad's physical appearance, dress, manners, family life, and daily conduct.
Article 4
Muhammad: Mercy to Mankind
An exploration of how the Prophet Muhammad embodied mercy in his dealings with his family, companions, opponents, and all of creation.
Article 5
Prophet Muhammad: A Pioneer of the Environment
A closer look at the Prophet Muhammad's extraordinary teachings on environmental stewardship, conservation, animal welfare, and sustainable use of natural resources.
Article 6
The Prophet's Compassion for Children
The Prophet Muhammad demonstrated extraordinary love and care for children, transforming the norms of his society to protect and honor the youngest members of the community.
Article 7
Prophet Muhammad in the Old Testament
An examination of Biblical passages that Muslim scholars believe contain references to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
Article 8
Prophet Muhammad in the New Testament
An examination of New Testament passages, particularly Jesus's promise of the Paraclete, which Muslim scholars interpret as references to the Prophet Muhammad.
Article 9
The Last Sermon of the Prophet Muhammad
The farewell sermon delivered by Prophet Muhammad during his final pilgrimage — a proclamation of human rights, equality, and universal brotherhood that echoes through the centuries.
Article 10
The Prophet of Islam: His Biography
A comprehensive overview of the life of Prophet Muhammad — from his birth in Mecca to his migration to Medina and the final years of his life.
