Prophet Muhammad in the New Testament

An examination of New Testament passages, particularly Jesus's promise of the Paraclete, which Muslim scholars interpret as references to the Prophet Muhammad.

In the Gospel of John, Jesus Christ (peace be upon him) promises the coming of 'the Paraclete' (or Comforter/Counselor) after his departure: 'And I will pray the Father, and He will give you another Helper, that he may abide with you forever.' (John 14:16)
